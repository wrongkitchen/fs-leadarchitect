Director
–
Simon Chua

Experience: Retail & entertainment mixed use architecture and interior design

Biography: Simon has over 20 years of experience in international design projects after graduating from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, and is a member of the Singapore Institute of Architects. Over the past 12 years he has worked predominantly in Asia, mainland China and Hong Kong on major design and construction projects. He is delighted to have joined a new company together with a team of colleagues that he has worked with for the past decade.

Prior to joining Lead, Simon worked for a major UK architectural firm, holding the post of Director in the Hong Kong office. He was responsible for several blue-chip clients, and implemented projects in Hong Kong, China, Jakarta and Taiwan. His varied experience allows him to work with several major regional and international developers. He manages a number of teams focusing on the day-to-day operations of the office and the delivery of innovative design solutions, whilst concurrently attending to finances and project deliverables.

Simon is active in design charettes and plays a part in advising government agencies on topics such as design and asset enhancement. Simon’s extensive design experience includes masterplanning, mixed-use developments, entertainment and interior design, high-end residential, hotels and transit interchange projects.

Simon previously led a number of projects including the newly completed 350,000m2 Shanghai World Trade Center, Shanghai IAPM mixed-use development (Sun Hung Kai Properties), Singapore ION Orchard Shopping Centre (CapitaLand), Central Plaza Bangkok, Taipei Dome Megacity, Chengdu IFS (Wharf), a major mixed-use development in Chengdu City (Hong Kong Land) and Venetian City (China Resources).

Likes: His family, music, food and a meaningful career
