Raymond Chan
–
Senior Architectural Designer

Experience: Mixed use developments, commercial architecture, and building façades.

Biography: Raymond is a Senior Architectural Designer at Lead and has over 6 years of experience. He is now collaborating with a team of talented architects and designers who have formed the core of Lead.

Raymond graduated from the City University of Hong Kong in 2008 with a bachelor’s degree in architecture and has worked in both international and local architectural practices since his graduation.

Before joining Lead, Raymond worked in an international architectural design firm as a Senior Architectural Designer. His responsibilities included assisting a team of architects in delivering design and drawing packages across a number of projects.

He has been a key member in many large-scale projects across Asia and his design experience includes master planning of mixed use developments, offices, hotels, serviced apartments, and building façades.

Raymond’s major built projects include the Dreamport shopping mall in Beijing, Huafa open retail area in Zhuhai, the Mercer Hotel in Hong Kong, and Excellence City in Shenzhen, which is currently under construction and will be completed by the end of 2014.
