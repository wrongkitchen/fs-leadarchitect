Ricky Tsang
–
Architectural Designer

Experience: Interior design, architecture and retail architecture.

Biography: Born in the UK, Ricky studied at the University of Westminster. After graduating he moved to Hong Kong where he worked for a well-known international firm.

He currently has 5 years of experience in retail architecture and his projects are mainly based around the ASEAN region with success such as Plaza Singapura, Dongmen 1234, Shenzhen, and Shanghai IAPM Mall.

His main focus is design and is constantly looking for innovation in the use of technology to help with the creative process.

Likes: Most sports, going out, the gym.

Thinks: ‘You’re only as good as your last piece of work’ Craig Menzies
