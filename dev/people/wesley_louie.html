Senior Associate Director

Experience: Architecture, masterplanning, concept design, mixed-use developments, retail architecture

Biography: Wesley is a Senior Associate Director at Lead and has over 10 years experience as a design architect, specialising in concept design and masterplanning across a broad range of building and project types.

He graduated from the University of Melbourne and is a registered Architect in Victoria, Australia and member of the Australian Institute of Architects. Along the way Wesley has gained considerable international experience at renowned commercial practices throughout Australia, Hong Kong and Singapore.

Prior to joining Lead, Wesley worked in a large international multi-disciplinary company in both its Hong Kong and Singapore offices. He was involved in many key projects within the Asia region, working on landmark mixed-use developments in Hong Kong, China, Philippines, India, Singapore and Malaysia. He was a key member of the competition winning team for a transport-orientated-development in Subang Jaya, Malaysia for major developer Sime Darby and was also responsible for leading the design on many key Indonesian commissions for experienced developer Agung Podomoro Land.

Other experience includes working on the recently opened Westgate lifestyle mall in Singapore (CapitaLand), early feasibility studies for the competition winning Terminal 4 in Singapore (Changi Airport Group), Vivocity Nanhai in Foshan, China (Mapletree), Suzhou CBD Tower & Suzhou Villa Masterplan in China (Sun Hung Kai Properties) and Fairview Terraces in Philippines (AyalaLand).

With his accumulated international experience and exposure to a wide selection of projects, Wesley remains passionate about design and strives to deliver creative solutions that are a balance between innovative design and commercial viability.
