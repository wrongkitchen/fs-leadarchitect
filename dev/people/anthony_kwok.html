Technical Associate
–
Kwok Ka Fai Antony

Experience: Façade and architectural design, BIM project management, Parametric 3D modeling and CG animation.

Biography: Antony is Lead’s Technical Associate Director and has 20 years of experience in Architectural design including an extensive knowledge of Digital Modelling, making him capable of handling projects of any complexity.

He graduated from the City University of Hong Kong, followed by the University of Queensland, Australia, and is a professional member of HKIBIM Association. He specialises in complex 3D form design, 3D visualization, façade design and BIM management.

Antony has worked for a couple of architectural firms in Hong Kong. Before joining Lead he worked for a Hong Kong branch of an international architectural firm as Senior Architectural Designer. Over the past eight years he has been involved in many mixed-use projects and Airport projects across Asia.

He has been involved in numerous major design projects such as Beijing Parkview, Beijing Capital International Airport competition, Shanghai ICC, Chongqing International Financial Centre, the new International Airport in Hyderabad, India. Also in Singapore ION Orchard, Plaza Singapura and Zhang Yi International Airport Terminal 4 building.
