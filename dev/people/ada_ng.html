Experience: Retail interior design, retail architecture and planning, commercial interior design

Biography: Ada is a Divisional Director at Lead and has over 10 years of experience in interior design. She has been involved in numerous award-winning projects and worked with renowned international developers and architects over the past 10 years. With her all-round skills and knowledge, Ada is capable of generating creative concepts, executing fine technical details and ultimately realising effective solutions.

Ada received training as an Architect at the University of Hong Kong and continued her studies in Stage Design in the UK. Having spent a few years practicing Event and Stage Setting in this glamorous industry she decided to apply her refreshing approach to the field of retail architecture.

Ada believes design is all about quality of life and that a designer creates experiences. With this approach she has successfully completed a number of prestigious retail projects, all of which are well known for the inclusion of human elements and hospitality; namely Hysan Place (HK), Elements (HK) and Shanghai IFC. These benchmark projects are well recognised by international awards and she has effectively established new standards in retail design.

Alongside her experience in retail architecture, she has worked on airport projects and commercial developments, such as the Jardine House Renovation, and interior designs for a number of office towers. Over the past 10 years Ada has built up her portfolio with experience working across Asia, including mainland China, Hong Kong, Korea, and Singapore.
