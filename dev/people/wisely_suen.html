Wisely Suen
–
Senior Associate Director

Experience: Retail planning, interior design, retail architecture, master planning, hotels, offices and serviced apartments, mixed use developments, airport enhancement

Biography: Wisely is a Senior Associate at Lead and has over 10 years of practical experience as a design architect, mainly specialising in concept design and planning.

He graduated from the University of New South Wales, Australia in 2009 and is a Hong Kong registered architect. Before joining the Lead team, Wisely worked in Hong Kong for a large multi-disciplinary international architectural firm in the role of Senior Associate Director and was responsible for projects in Hong Kong, China and Taiwan. He has previously worked with a portfolio of experienced developers such as Sun Hung Kai, KWG Thai Central Group, Taiwan’s Far Eastern Group and Far Glory Group.

Wisely has consulted on a variety of commercial and mixed-use development projects and has many years of senior experience in master planning, architecture and interior design. His creative flair and innovative approach to design has garnered him numerous accolades including his previous projects such as, ICC Sky 100 Observation Deck, Shanghai iAPM, Shanghai Arch residential, optimisation of Hong Kong International Airport, Foshan JiaZhou Square Mall, Jakarta’s LIPPO Group Plaza Semanggi optimization project, Far Glory Taipei Dome, Taiwan MegaCity and BigCity.
