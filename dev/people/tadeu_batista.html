Tadeu Batista
–
Architect

Experience: Retail design, residential and mixed-use developments

Biography: Tadeu is an architect at Lead and has over 5 years of international experience in design. He graduated from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro with a degree in Architecture and Urban Planning, followed by the University of Lisbon, Portugal and is a registered architect in Brazil.

Before moving to Hong Kong Tadeu worked in Brazil for two years, with involvement in a range of commercial and residential projects. Prior to Lead he worked for a Hong Kong branch of an international architectural firm, playing a key part in a variety of masterplanning, mixed use and retail projects in South East Asia and mainland China.

Over the past three years he has gained experience in many projects across Asia such as YIBD (Dreamhub) Seoul, KL Metropolis and Sunrise Solaris 3, both in Kuala Lumpur, Lan Kwai Fong Wuxi and the Grand Hyatt in Rio de Janeiro.

Likes: Traveling and exploring, kitesurfing and water sports, eating BBQ.

Thinks: ‘É melhor arrepender-se por ter feito alguma coisa do que por não ter feito nada.’
