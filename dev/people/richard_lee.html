Richard Lee
–
Senior Associate Director

Experience: Retail planning, retail architecture and residential developments

Biography: Richard is currently heading our growing Kuala Lumpur Design Studio in Malaysia, his hometown. He feels great to be back home after spending more than a decade studying and working abroad. He has strong passion to make his country a beautiful and sustainable place through his valuable international exposure and design experience.

Richard graduated from the Oxford Brookes University in the UK, and then worked in Oxford for a design practice, which specialised in institutional developments and heritage building regeneration. He is a registered architect in the UK and also a member of the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA).

His work experience prior to Lead was with a large multi-disciplinary design practice with landmark projects in Kuala Lumpur.

Likes: Playing squash, jungle hiking and shopping. Coffee everyday - no milk or sugar please!
