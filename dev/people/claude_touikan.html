Claude Touikan
–
Director

Experience: Site Planning, architectural planning, architectural design

Biography: Claude was born in Madagascar, grew up in Stanley, Hong Kong, and studied in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. During his 28 year career to date he has worked in Montreal, Edmonton, Macau and Hong Kong. He has directed design teams in North America and Asia and is a member of the Royal Architectural Institute of Canada.

Prior to Lead, Claude held the role of Director in a large muti-disciplinary UK practice, heading a team dedicated to project work across mainland China. Claude has been involved in and provided conceptual design advice for several major construction projects, including iAPM, ICC Pudong and Shanghai IFC, Yueda 889 Shanghai, Beijing Dreamport (Oak Bay) and the Guiyang Garland (flower garden).

Claude is driven to provide creative and cost effective solutions, with experience in negotiating on government project tendering in China in complex design competitions and acquisition awards. Claude believes that sustainable design and community integration are key influences of future Lead projects. Cooperating with developers on these points is pivotal in delivering a successful project for his clients across the region.
