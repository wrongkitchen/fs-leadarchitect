Christine Hau

Experience: International experience in finance and management, commercial and physical expansions of architectural practices in Asia

Biography: Christine Hau is a Director with Lead in Hong Kong and has over 17 years of international experience in finance and management, leading the commercial and physical expansion of architectural practices in Asia for the last 12 years.

Prior to Lead, Christine was the Finance Director for a major international architectural company where she was responsible for leading the finance and operational division alongside facilitating the company’s growth plan. Her role included undertaking audits, taxation, strategies, legal and compliance issues.

Also in this time Christine was instrumental in the opening of 6 offices across the Asia region – Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, Singapore, Malaysia and India – with a total capacity of around 400 staff.

On top of her former experience Christine is accomplished in structuring solutions, methodologies and financial transactions in favour with commercial and cross-border arrangements for architectural practices. Key to enabling this is her knowledge of finance and taxation along with her experience in complex restructuring, international expansion and capital market transactions.

She achieved a track record of documented contributions that led the company’s improved financial performance, heightened productivity and enhanced internal control for the management.

Christine is currently looking after the Lead corporate division to ensure the legal, operational and financial objectives remain in line with the projected growth of the business.
