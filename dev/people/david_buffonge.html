David Buffonge
–
Director

Experience: Retail planning, architecture, design, urban design and master planning

Biography: David is an award winning architect and urban designer with over 18 years of experience in the design of large mixed-use developments around the world. He enjoyed a successful career in London before moving to Hong Kong in 2002.

David’s passions for shaping the built environment and his awareness of the contextual influences of a location have contributed to his involvement in the design of many prominent buildings throughout Asia. His designs and projects have, in many cases, taken on national importance within their respective countries. His portfolio of work includes the highly acclaimed Elements Mall in Hong Kong, and the award winning mixed use development ION Orchard & The Orchard Residences in Singapore.

Prior to joining Lead, David was the Design Director of a large British architectural practice working with a dynamic team to spearhead their international design portfolio covering the UK, Europe, Middle East and Asia. David was also responsible for leading the design of Changi Airport’s New Boutique Terminal 4, currently under construction and scheduled for completion in 2017.

David has been a speaker at numerous conferences throughout Asia. He is an expert in Retail Planning, Architecture & Design, Urban Design & Master Planning, High Density Vertical Mixed-Use Development, Transport Oriented Design (TOD) & Airport Terminals.
