Xing Wang
–
Architect

Experience: Architectural design and retail planning

Biography: Xing Wang is an architect at Lead and has over 5 years of international experience in architectural design and urban planning. He continues to be involved in many important retail projects.

Xing is also a registered architect in the Netherlands, having graduated from the Delft University of Technology with an honourable masters degree.

Before joining the Lead team he used to work as an urban planner in China and was involved in many large residential, tourism and university planning projects. After graduating from his masters he worked for a Dutch design firm as an architect and engineer.

After moving to Hong Kong he secured a role working for a large multi-disciplinary international architectural firm as a Senior Architectural Designer, being responsible for many Chinese retail development projects.

 

A selection of Xing’s previous design projects include the Dongguan Vanke Plaza (Vanke), Guiyang Huaguoyuan Commercial Center (Honglicheng Group) and Beijing’s K11 Mall (New World).

Likes: Chinese calligraphy and running, both of which bring peace and happiness.
