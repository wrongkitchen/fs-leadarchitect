Experience: Concept design, retail planning, interior design, retail architecture, creation of artwork and sculptural installations, construction knowledge, construction detailing, product knowledge, material knowledge, supply line knowledge, mechanical and electrical experience, lighting design, graphic and signage design.

Biography: Chris grew up in the UK and studied Three Dimensional design at Leeds Metropolitan University. Since graduating in 1992 Chris has worked for a number of high profile architectural practices including Studios Architecture, Gensler, Aedas and Benoy.

His career has taken him from the UK to the US and finally Asia, where he resides, now working as a Director at Lead. Chris continues to collaborate on projects large and small with the colleagues he has known for the past 10 years, and who now form Lead. He will pursue his passion for design in all its fields wherever it takes him.

Likes: Chris lives in the Country Park enclave of Hong Kong and relishes the outdoors with his family there. He is a keen cyclist and enjoys suffering on the hills of Hong Kong, Spain and Italy. The captivating world of design is never far from his consciousness.
