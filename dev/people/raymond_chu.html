Raymond Chu
–
Senior Architect

Experience: Master planning, commercial architecture, façade design

Biography: Born and raised in Hong Kong, Raymond spent his teenage years in Essex, UK before returning to Hong Kong for his architectural studies. He graduated from the University of Hong Kong with a masters degree in architecture, and is a registered architect here.

Liaising with Local Design Institutes (LDI’s) to produce submission packages has allowed Raymond to familiarise himself with local building codes, and also design that meets the demand of local property markets. His design experience includes master planning mixed-use developments, architectural design including offices, retail, hotels and residential towers.

Raymond’s major built projects include Raffles City Phase II in Shenzhen, C&D Headquarters and retail mall in Xiamen and Garden City Cyber Port in Shenzhen. Of recent note is his work on Excellence City in Shenzhen, which is currently under construction and will be completed by the end of 2014.

Likes: Swimming, cycling and running. He also has 2 cats.

Thinks: Live each day as if it’s your last, because one day it certainly will be.
