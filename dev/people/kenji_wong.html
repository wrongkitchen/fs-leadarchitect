Kenji Wong
–
Associate Director

Experience: Retail planning, retail architecture, office architecture, mixed-used development planning, interior design, masterplanning

Biography: Kenji is an Associate Director at Lead and has 10 years of international experience in design. He continues to be instrumental in a number of landmark projects.

He specialises in unique and creative design ideas coupled with his specialist knowledge of construction programing scripts, which allows him to apply unique conceptual ideas to each project.

Kenji has won numerous design awards across several international architectural design competitions. Graduating from the University of Hong Kong he then worked in London for an internationally renowned design practice. He’s a member of the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA) and now also the Hong Kong Institute of Architects (HKIA), being a Hong Kong registered architect.

His work experience prior to Lead was with a large multi-disciplinary design practice with landmark projects in Mainland China, Taiwan and Singapore. Over the years, Kenji has led design teams in collaboration with major local and international developers to create unique, innovative and iconic projects. He has experience in masterplanning commercial offices, hotels and complex mixed-use projects, building façades and interior design. Kenji’s previous design projects include Shenzhen Mini Mix-C (China Resources Land), Guangzhou SHK Parc Central, Singapore Westgate office tower and shopping mall (CapitaLand), Chinatrust Taipei (China Trust Commercial Bank) and Hsinchu High Speed Rail Retail Park.
