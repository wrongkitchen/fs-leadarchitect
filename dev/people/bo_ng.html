Bo Ng
–
Senior Interior Designer

Experience: Interior design, retail planning

Biography: Bo is a Senior Interior Designer at Lead and has over 7 years of practical experience in her discipline. She is now collaborating with a team of talented architects and designers who have formed the core of Lead.

Bo has been a key member in numerous award-winning projects across Asia, working with renowned international developers and architects over the past seven years. Her experience gives her the ability to see the whole design process from concept to construction, making her a true all-round designer.

Bo graduated from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. With a background in interior design, she developed her unique aesthetic and design sense, which has been the inspiration for many award-winning projects. She believes design is about creating character and that this character gives life to her designs. She continues to develop her creative thinking on a wide range of projects at Lead.

Prior to joining Lead, Bo practiced in the Hong Kong office of an international architectural firm where she was responsible for the design and execution of high profile projects in Hong Kong, Mainland China and Korea. Key projects include Pudong IFC in Shanghai, Hysan Place in Hong Kong and Mix C in Qing Dao.
