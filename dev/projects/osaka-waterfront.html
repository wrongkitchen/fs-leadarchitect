Osaka Waterfront Development
Designs for a prominent resort, retail and entertainment development
A forward thinking new master plan set to transform a prime waterfront site in Osaka into a new destination for tourism and entertainment.
Lead were appointed to create a new retail driven masterplan for this development, compromising a hotel resort, business park and retail precinct, for the city’s new waterfront development.

Located in a prominent location on the waterfront, the proposed 900,000sqm development creates a new entertainment and retail hub for the city and connects with the main transport network.
Project Facts
Location Osaka, Japan
GFA: 900,000sqm
Appointed: 2014
Expected completion: 2018

Our Role
Masterplanning, retail design and branding
