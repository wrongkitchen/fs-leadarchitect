iAPM
A world class retail and entertainment destination, in the heart of Shanghai’s Huai Hai Road shopping district
Creating a unique destination that feeds off one of Shanghai’s busiest shopping streets
There were a lot of opportunities on the table at the start of the iAPM project, but as ever it was how they were combined that truly made a difference. Some of the key design moves included:
  • Creation of an L2 Office and Residential address drop-off on the south side to allow strong retail frontages on the Huai Hai road street frontage
  • Worked in close collaboration with the local Shanghai planners to create a reduced street set-back to help retain the Huai Hai Road shopping street character
  • Use of the external roof levels facing Nanjing Road to activate 3 levels of F&B terraces allowing for roof top dining experiences with city wide views
  • close collaboration with the city MTR company to create integrated station entrances to the B2 Retail levels
  • Utilising B1 and B2 sunken courts to contribute to the Fire Safety plan

Project Facts
Client: Sun Hung Kai Properties
GFA: 110,000sqm
Levels: 7
Designed: 2008 – 2012
Completed: 2013
