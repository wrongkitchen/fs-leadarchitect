Mixed Use Masterplan
Lead's first appointment for a resort project
Lead have originated initial masterplan concepts for a mixed use lifestyle centre, as part of a larger commercial and resort project.
The concept for the resort envisages the use of a connecting water body, and a combination of covered spaces, streets and squares to deliver a rich mix of experiences to the customer.
Project Facts
Client: Confidential
Location: Confidential (Asia)
GFA: approx. 50,000sqm
Levels: 3–4
Appointed: 2014
Expected completion: TBC

Our Role
Masterplanning, retail planning

Project Team
