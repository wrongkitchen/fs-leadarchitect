Song Lei Department Store
A brand new department store experience for the city of Harbin
Eight levels of experiential retail, from high end to cutting edge, housed within a rich architectural shell.
Lead are designing a two phase development combining high end luxury brands contained within the restored shell of a heritage building, and the second a new build extension that will house on trend fashion stores.

With its conceptual essence interwoven with the buildings classic art nouveaux architectural heritage this department store is designed with flowing organic geometrical features carefully tailored to keep the shopper exploring.

With seven levels above ground and one basement each floor has its own unique personality of materials and textures. The experience is augmented at level seven with a Cineplex and specialist food and beverage tenants, creating an anchor at high-level while the basement dedicates a major space for an all-day foodcourt facility.
Project Facts
Client: Songlei Industrial Group
GFA: 68,000sqm
Levels: 7 above ground, 1 below
Appointed: April 2014
Completion: TBA
