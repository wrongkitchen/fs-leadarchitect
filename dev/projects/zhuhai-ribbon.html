Zhuhai Ribbon
Reviewing and reconstructing the way customers experience this retail development
The results of the audits and research led to a unique concept where the end users become a pivotal part of the space.
Lead have conducted a full audit of the clients exiting interior master plan and have re-designed the interior spaces to create a more enhanced experience.

The separated building elements have been linked to create a more seamless flow through the dining and retail offers.

A ribbon like element has been introduced in the design, which links the spaces and encourages exploration.
Project Facts
Client: Huafa Zhuhai
Location: Zhuhai, Guangdong, PRC
GFA: 30,000sqm
Levels: 6
Appointed: 2014
Expected completion: 2015

Our Role
Interior design and retail planning
