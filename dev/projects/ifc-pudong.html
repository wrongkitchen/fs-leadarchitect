IFC Pudong
A world class retail and entertainment destination, in the heart of Shanghai’s Huai Hai Road shopping district
Designed to be very much 2 malls in one, appealing to a broader audience and creating a unique experience
IFC was aimed at 2 contrasting audiences: above ground a luxury offer, and below ground a different set of spaces and retail lifestyle units to appeal more to a younger market. Some of the key design moves included:
  • Creation of a distinct Luxury village on the route from the main atrium to the Ritz Carlton hotel
  • Collaboration with the graphics team focused on the IFC brand ‘leitmotif’ resulting in the creation of a concept evoking interesting textural qualities within the design
  • Use of the external roof levels to activate F&B clusters allowing for pleasurable rooftop dining experiences with citywide views
  • Close co-operation with the retailer Apple to conceptualise their glass cylinder flagship plaza store
  • Utilising B1 sunken courts to contribute to the Fire Safety plan

Project Facts
Client: Sun Hung Kai
GFA: 90,000sqm
Levels: 6
Designed: 2005 – 2009
Completed: 2010
