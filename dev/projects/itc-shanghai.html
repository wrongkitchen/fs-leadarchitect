ITC Shanghai
Large scale lifestyle urban destination hub
An understanding of the broader context was a key driver for the conceptual ideas we explored for this project.
An urban design approach for this large scale mixed use site saw the creation of streets and spaces which compliment the indoor retail zones.
Key consideration has been given to the wider masterplan and neighbourhood linkages to adjacent buildings, ensuring integration with the urban fabric of the district.
Project Facts
Client: Sun Hung Kai Properties
Location: Shanghai, PRC
GFA: 220,000sqm
Levels: 2/3 basement levels, and 4-5 storeys above ground
Appointed: 2014
Expected completion: TBC

Our Role
Architecture and retail planning
